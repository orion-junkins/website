Built on a custom path tracer developed during ETH's Advanced Computer Graphics course.
Features include RIS-based direct illumination, ReSTIR DI, and a physically accurate camera model with lens effects.

Awarded [2nd place](https://cgl.ethz.ch/teaching/cg24/competition/competition.php) in the 2024 ETH + Disney Research Rendering Competition.
