A geometry-based pixel compatibility heuristic for guiding spatial neighbor selection in ReSTIR via weighted reservoir sampling.
Reduces SMAPE by 6--29% and temporal covariance by 22--49% at 2--5% overhead, improving the Pareto frontier of ReSTIR's error--correlation tradeoff.

**Authors:** O. Junkins, M. Kettunen, D. Lin, R. Ramamoorthi, C. Wyman

**Venue:** PACMCGIT 9(4), 2026 — to appear at [HPG 2026](https://highperformancegraphics.org/2026/)
