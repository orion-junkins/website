A stacked ensemble of spatially distributed GRUs for short-term river flow forecasting.
Outperformed NOAA 24-hour forecasts by 2.85% MAPE.
Extended into [LevelSage](https://github.com/orion-junkins/river-level-forecasting), a full-stack system running inference every 6 hours for 15 rivers on AWS at under $0.25 per model per month.

**Authors:** P. J. Donnelly, O. Junkins

**Venue:** IEEE ICFSP 2022, Paris — [View on IEEE](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9924877) · [GitHub](https://github.com/orion-junkins/river-level-forecasting) · [Presentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s9dqKgClDeo)
